Donor Registration

Eligibility Requirements

  • You must be at least 18 years old and weigh more than 45 kg.
  • You must be in good health and feeling well on the day of donation.
  • You must not have donated blood in the past 3 months (for men) or 4 months (for women).
  • You must not have consumed alcohol or drugs within the last 24 hours.
  • You must not be suffering from infectious diseases (HIV, Hepatitis, Malaria, etc.).
  • You must not have undergone major surgery or tattooing within the past 6 months.
  • You must not be pregnant or recently given birth (if female).
  • Your hemoglobin level should be within the normal range.
