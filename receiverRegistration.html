Receiver Registration

Eligibility Requirements

  • Receiver must have a valid medical reason for requesting blood.
  • A hospital reference or patient ID should be available if needed.
  • Receiver’s contact and hospital details must be accurate and verifiable.
  • Receiver must not be involved in any illegal or commercial blood trade.
  • The request must be genuine and supported by valid medical documentation if necessary.
  • Blood will be used only for legitimate medical treatment purposes.
